About CureOil

CureOil has been established since April 2017 by Nicole Campbell, the designer of this website. We sell a variety of oils for hair and skin without any chemical processes nor additives. For hair, our oils promote growth, itch and dandruff control. For skin, our oils relieve itching and continuous dry skin. Also, try our oil diffuser for aroma therapy. Our natural products are raw and freshly made from the seeds and leaves from various different places worldwide, including here in America. Come shop and enjoy our natural healing process!
